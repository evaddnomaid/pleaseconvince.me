PleaseConvince.Me

The Problem(s)

  • Politics is ugly.
  • Participants typically band together with people who already agree with them.
  • When not in their own echo chamber, participants shout slogans past each other. No one is listening.
  • It's rare that anyone changes their mind. About anything.
  • When you seek information on an issue/candidate, you'll probably receive canned sound bites from sources with vested interest in their agenda.
  • Most available information is heavily polarized / partisan.
  • I don't know what will be on my upcoming ballot.
  • I don't have any idea how I should vote for issue or candidate X.
  • "Why would anyone vote for/against issue/candidate X? Everyone I know is voting the way I am, and everyone on the other side of this issue/candidate just wants to yell at me."

The Proposal

  • Create a resource where people who are seeking to educate themselves can contact other people who:
    • know and care about issue/candidate X when I don't (yet) have an opinion.
    • are (probably) voting differently than I am once I do have an opinion.

We can all become more educated and thoughtful in our politics by intentionally and respectfully seeking the thoughts of people who are educated, opinionated, and disagree with our own views.

PleaseConvince.Me IS NOT

  • A place to post your boilerplate.
  • A place to be disrespectful to others.
  • A spammer resource.
  • A place to gang up together in a private echo chamber.
